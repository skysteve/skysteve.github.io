Steve Jenkins

Senior Software Engineer

{% include picture-face.html %}

Born in the Isle of Man in the early 90s Steve moved to Newcastle Upon Tyne in 2008 for University before moving to Vancouver, BC, Canada in 2018


As a Software Engineer, Steve began writing code professionally in 2012. Since then he's specialised in web development focusing mainly on the frontend while having reasonable experience with Node.js.


Currently based in Vancouver, Canada working for Tile, as a senior software engineeer, Steve works on a mix of frontend and backend projects mostly with React.js and the odd bit of Ruby


In his spare time, Steve likes to tinker with home automation projects and developing progressive web apps using Web Components.


Steve also enjoys paragliding, especially back home in the Isle of Man. Kitesurfing, Kayaking, Hiking, Climbing and generally being outside!
